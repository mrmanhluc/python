3.2 Standard deviation and Variance¶

\begin{align} SD = \frac{1}{N - 1} \sum_{i=0}^{N} (x_i - \bar{x})^2 * P(x) \end{align}

\begin{align} Var = \sqrt{SD} \end{align}
